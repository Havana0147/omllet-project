SIMPLE OMLLET RECIPE

An easy and perfect dish for any meal. This classic omllet combi nes beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choices of cheese, vegetables or meat

Preparation time

  • Total: approximatley 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

Ingridents

  • 2-3 eggs large
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 table teaspoon
  • optonal fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. cook the omllet: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in te eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure ensure the eggs evenly coat tge surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edgets but are still slightly ruuny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over over one of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette contiues to cook, cook carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fiilings. let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
